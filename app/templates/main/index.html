What is Maps4All?

Maps4All is an open source platform for organizations to display their resources and other information on a map. For example, one of our first clients, YouthMattersPhilly created a Maps4All instance with all the resources for foster youth outlined on a map which was filterable, searchable, and crowdsourced.
